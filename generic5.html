NBA COVID-19 Analyzer

The NBA COVID-19 Analyzer was a way to investigate how COVID-19 Affected the NBA. I worked with 2 other undergraduate students throughout this project. I was mainly involved in data collection, analysis, and visualization aspects of this project. I started off scraping data from ESPN using Python and BeautifulSoup for the Top 50 Offensive NBA Players. I compared the players' performances during the pre-COVID-19 and COVID-19 Affected Season ("Bubble") with NumPy, Pandas, and Scikit-Learn. V The data was visualized using Python libraries, such as Matplotlib and Seaborn. Overall, this was fully developed in the Jupyter notebook environment following a QQQ Reporting structure starting with a Hypothesis and leading to a Conclusion. Eventually, after thorough analysis, it was concluded that the players performed better during the COVID-19 affected season.
